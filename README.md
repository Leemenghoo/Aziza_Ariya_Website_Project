# Ariya — Puzzle Game Website

Welcome to Ariya! This is an interactive jigsaw puzzle game where you can upload your own images and create custom puzzles to play.

## What is Ariya?

Ariya is a fun, hand-drawn puzzle game where you can:
- Upload any image you like
- Choose puzzle difficulty and shape
- Play the puzzle by dragging pieces
- Add decorative frames to your finished puzzle
- Download your completed puzzle

## How to Use

### Step 1: Upload Your Image
1. Click **"Play"** on the home page
2. Click **"Choose Image"** button
3. Select a photo from your device
4. The image will be previewed on the screen

### Step 2: Configure Your Puzzle
1. Choose your **puzzle grid** (Easy 4×4, Medium 6×6, Hard 8×8, Expert 12×12)
2. Select your **puzzle shape** (Jigsaw or Standard)
3. Select **difficulty level** (Easy, Medium, Hard, Expert)
4. Click **"Start Game"** to begin

### Step 3: Play the Puzzle
1. **Drag puzzle pieces** with your mouse to the correct location
2. Pieces will **snap together** when placed correctly
3. Use the **Hint button** for help (limited hints available)
4. Watch the **timer** and **move counter**
5. When all pieces are correctly placed, you win!

### Step 4: Add Frame (Optional)
After completing the puzzle, you can:
1. Choose a decorative **frame style**
2. Customize frame settings if available

### Step 5: Download Your Puzzle
1. Click the **"Download"** button
2. Your completed puzzle will be saved as an image file
3. Share it with friends or keep it as a memory!

## Tips for Best Results

- Use clear, colorful images for easier puzzles
- Start with easier difficulty levels if you're new to puzzles
- Use hints wisely (you have a limited number)
- Larger images work better than very small ones
- Try different puzzle shapes for variety
