Behind the Lines

Our story and why we love puzzles.

Welcome to Ariya, a playful corner of the internet dedicated to the simple joy of putting things back together. In a world of polished pixels and corporate gradients, we wanted to build something that feels tactile, hand-crafted, and slightly wobbly.

Our Vision

We believe every photo tells a story. By breaking them into pieces and inviting you to rebuild them, we hope to give you a moment of focus, a touch of whimsy, and a new way to appreciate your favorite memories.

Ariya is built with 100% client-side technology. We don't store your photos, we don't track your moves beyond the session, and we certainly don't ask for a login. It's just you, your browser, and a pile of sketch-style jigsaw pieces.

No Backend

Privacy first. Everything stays on your device.

Pure Vanilla

No frameworks. Just raw HTML, CSS, and JS.
