Behind the Lines

Our story and why we love puzzles.

Welcome to Ariya. This website is a special project created to help Ariya, a student from Aziza's Place in Phnom Penh, Cambodia, learn modern web development.

The Story

Aziza's Place is a nonprofit learning and development center for underprivileged children in Cambodia. Above and Beyond School partnered with Aziza's Place to provide free Python education to the students there.

While Python is important, we wanted to help Ariya see that technology can create something visual and interactive. So we taught her how to use AI tools in a professional development environment to build a complete website. This project was built using AI-assisted prompt writing in VS Code, giving Ariya real experience with modern development workflows.

The mission was simple: help Ariya stay motivated in her learning journey by showing her that she could create something real, something people could use, and something she could share with the world.

Our Vision

Ariya is a playful puzzle game where every photo tells a story. By breaking them into pieces and inviting you to rebuild them, we hope to give you a moment of focus, a touch of whimsy, and a new way to appreciate your favorite memories.

Ariya is built with 100% client-side technology. We don't store your photos, we don't track your moves beyond the session, and we certainly don't ask for a login. It's just you, your browser, and a pile of sketch-style jigsaw pieces.

No Backend

Privacy first. Everything stays on your device.

Pure Vanilla

No frameworks. Just raw HTML, CSS, and JS.
